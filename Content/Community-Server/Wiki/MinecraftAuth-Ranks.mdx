---
title: MinecraftAuth Ranks
---

The Community Server uses MinecraftAuth for authorization, requiring players to link their Microsoft accounts to 3rd party services to get either access to the server or ranks once inside of it. You can find a list of the ranks and what services are used to verify that the player should have it below.

<table>

<tr>
<th>Rank</th>
<th>Service</th>
<th>Details</th>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>Twitch Subscriber</td>
<td>Twitch</td>
<td>Must be a subscriber on Twitch</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>Twitch Follower</td>
<td>Twitch</td>
<td>Must be a follower on Twitch</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>Patron</td>
<td>Patreon</td>
<td>Must be a Patron on Patreon</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>Speaker</td>
<td>Discord</td>
<td>The speaker role is given at level 10 by CarlBot</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>HeartWood</td>
<td>Discord</td>
<td>Manually granted to long-term and active members</td>
</tr>

<tr>
<td>YouTube Subscriber</td>
<td>YouTube</td>
<td>Must be subscribed to my primary YouTube channel <code>@n-aspen</code></td>
</tr>

</table>
